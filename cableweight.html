Notes:
  • Cable weights are approximate and vary by manufacturer
  • Armoured cables include steel wire armour (SWA) or aluminium wire armour
  • Values based on XLPE/PVC insulated cables with PVC outer sheath
  • Use for ordering, transport planning, and cable support design
  • Actual weights should be confirmed with manufacturer datasheets
Typical Applications:
  • Material Ordering: Calculate total cable weight for procurement and delivery logistics
  • Cable Support Design: Determine support spacing and capacity per AS/NZS 3000 Table 4.1
  • Transport & Handling: Plan lifting equipment and drum sizes for installation
  • Cable Tray Loading: Calculate tray loading to ensure compliance with manufacturer specifications
  • Cost Estimation: Weight-based freight calculations for project budgeting
Reference Standards:
  • AS/NZS 3000 - Electrical installations (Wiring Rules) - Table 4.1: Cable support spacing
  • AS/NZS 3008.1.1 - Electrical installations - Selection of cables (cable specifications)
  • AS/NZS 5000.1 - Electric cables - Polymeric insulated (cable construction standards)
  • Manufacturer datasheets for specific cable products and exact weights
