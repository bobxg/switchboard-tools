Main Tools

  • Switchboard Sizer Design and visualize MSB layouts with drag-and-drop modules. Calculates dimensions and generates printable schematics.
  • Switchroom Sizer Visual switchroom layout with working clearances, equipment placement, and dimensional checks.
  • Cable Tray Sizer Cable tray width and depth selection per AS/NZS 3000 cl. 3.9 and AS 3013, with derating factors.
  • Lighting Search Search lighting products from approved Australian manufacturers. Filter by multiple suppliers simultaneously.

Power

  • Cable Sizing Cable selection per AS/NZS 3008.1.1 with current capacity, voltage drop, and earth fault ratings.
  • Cable Weight Calculate cable weights for structural and containment sizing.
  • Maximum Demand Maximum demand calculator per AS/NZS 3000 Appendix C for domestic and commercial installations.
  • Diversity Factors AS/NZS 3000 Appendix C diversity factor lookup for domestic, commercial, and industrial loads.
  • Fault Level Calculator Prospective fault current from transformer to downstream nodes per IEC 60909 / AS/NZS 3000.
  • Loop Impedance Earth fault loop impedance Zs calculator per AS/NZS 3000 cl. 2.6.2–2.6.3.
  • Discrimination Check Selectivity verification between upstream and downstream protective devices.
  • Transformer Sizing Transformer kVA selection with losses, impedance, and fault current estimation per AS 2374.
  • Generator Sizing Generator kVA sizing with step-loading sequence per AS/NZS 3010:2017.
  • UPS Sizing UPS kVA and battery Ah calculator per AS 4044:2019.
  • PFC Sizing Power factor correction capacitor bank sizing with harmonic resonance check.
  • Harmonics Analysis THD estimation for LED/VSD-heavy installations per AS/NZS 61000.3 series.
  • Busbar Sizing Copper/aluminium busbar current capacity and short-circuit withstand per AS/NZS 61439.
  • CT Sizing Current transformer ratio, accuracy class, and burden calculation per AS 1284.1 / AS 60044-1.
  • Earthing Calculator Earth electrode resistance for rods, strips, grids, and plates using Laurent-Niemann formulas.
  • PV Sizing Solar PV system sizing and inverter selection per AS/NZS 5033.
  • RCD Selector RCD type and sensitivity selection per AS/NZS 3000 cl. 2.6.3–2.6.4.
  • SPD Sizing Surge protection device risk assessment and selection per AS/NZS 1768:2021.
  • IP/IK Rating Guide IP and IK rating decoder with environment-based recommendations per AS 60529 / AS 62262.
  • Conduit Sizer Conduit fill calculations for various cable types and configurations.

Lighting

  • Lux Levels Reference Searchable database of required illuminance levels per AS/NZS 1680.

  • Fire Interface Matrix Fire cause-and-effect matrix per AS 1670.1, AS 1668.1, and NCC.
  • Smoke Control Smoke control electrical requirements and power estimation per AS 1668.1.
  • Essential Services Essential safety measures register with maintenance schedules per NCC Section E.
