NSW DNSP Metering Position Standards

Position dimensions and arrangement rules sourced from Ausgrid NUS 080, Endeavour Energy Metering Cubicle Standard, and Essential Energy Customer Installation Requirements. These standards specify minimum requirements — the metering cubicle manufacturer must provide type-tested enclosures to AS/NZS 61439.1.
